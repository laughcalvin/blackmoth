Bio

Thomas Fec, better known by his stage name Tobacco, is an American electronic musician. He releases music as a solo artist as well as the frontman of the psychedelic dreampop band Black Moth Super Rainbow.

He, along with the rest of Black Moth Super Rainbow, is very private and rarely does interviews, so very little is known about him. He grew up in Allegheny County, Pennsylvania and has stated his name derived from "a character that freaked me out as a kid, the Tobacco Man," in reference to the character from the film Redneck Zombies.

Style

"Mostly everything I've done with BMSR is made to be pop. And alot [sic] of people say BMSR is bordering on hip-hop beats. So with Tobacco, I wanted to embrace my beats and get darker and sleeker with it all. I want to make you feel paranoid in a good way. There’s something seriously fucked about workout tapes from the mid 80s, and just about everything obscure on beta tape. They make me feel awful, but really good and curious at the same time. With this Tobacco stuff, I’m trying to translate that feeling."
